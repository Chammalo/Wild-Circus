About Us

Wild Circus is founded in 1995, by Tartampion Familly.

We start as a little and familly circus, and grow up with, new spectacular performances made by great artists, like the tightrope walker Ben Tartampion ans is wife Gina Dupont, or the clown Arthur Fleck, and this is only a little part of our recognized artists, because we are a big familly of 14 peoples. We have animals too. A wonderfull little lama, named Kuzco (Only when he doesn't spit ahah), and a Lion, named Patapouf because he's a little bit lazzy, and other animals, but you have to come to discover the other. Oh and don't worry they are well treated, and we give to them all the love that we can give.

At the begining we only have representations in France, but now we travel in many countries, for sure in France, but in Belgium, and Swiss as well.

Don't hesitate to send us a little message, it will be a pleasure to read you. Hope you will see soon in our show.
